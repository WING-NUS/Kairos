Workshop programme

The full-day workshop on the 28th of June, 2009 features nine paper presentations and one invited talk by Dr Sihem Amer-Yahia of Yahoo! Research.  The workshop will be held in the Rhode Island Convention Center, Room 556A.

8:15-8:45 Registration 
8:45-9:00 Opening remarks 
9:00-10:00 Keynote speech
  • Information Presentation of Ranked Datasets. Sihem Amer-Yahia, Yahoo! Research. 
10:00-10:30 Coffee break 
10:30-12:00 Research session 1: Quality and provenance
  • Enhancing a Data Cleaning Process using Repairs. Emanuel Santos.
  • Computing Query Answers with Consistent Support - A Ph.D. project proposal. Jui-Yi Kao.
  • Protecting the Past and Present of Data with Applications in Provenance and Regulatory Compliant Databases. Ragib Hasan.
12:00-13:30 Lunch break
13:30-15:00 Research session 2: New data management paradigms
  • Complex Event Detection in Pervasive Computing. Chunjie Zhou and Xiaofeng Meng.
  • Efficient Similarity Search on Distribution Data. Yasuko Matsubara, Yasushi Sakurai and Masatoshi Yoshikawa.
  • Goal-Driven Autonomous Database Tuning Supported by a System Model. Karsten Schmidt.
15:00-15:30 Coffee break 
15:30-17:00 Research session 3: Query processing
  • SkyDB: Skyline Aware Query Evaluation Framework. Venkatesh Raghavan and Elke Rundensteiner.
  • Achieving High Output Utility under Limited Resources through Structure-based Shedding and Spilling in XML Streams. Mingzhu Wei, Elke Rundensteiner and Murali Mani.
  • Keyword and Natural Language Query Processing for Semi-Structured Data Sources. Arash Termehchy.
17:00-17:15 Closing remarks
