Want to do an impromptu poster?

If you've not contacted us before, but you have something you are burning to show, feel free to bring an poster no bigger than A2 in size. We'll show you where to put it. We've got spaces around the edges, and that's what wikis (and this wikiconference) is for: Innovating.
