Accepted Submissions

ICWE Call for Papers has attracted a total of 90 submissions from all over the world with a good coverage of all the different aspects of Web Engineering. All submitted paper were reviewed in detailed by a program committee composed from experts in this domain. Based on their reviews, 22 submissions were accepted as full papers (24%) and 15 as short papers (22%). Apart from that, 8 posters and 10 demos complete the program of ICWE 2009.
