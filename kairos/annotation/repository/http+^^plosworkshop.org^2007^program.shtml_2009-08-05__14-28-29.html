Presentation
09:00 - 09:15 Welcome
09:15 - 10:30 Session 1: "Construction and Repair"

04:00 - 05:15 Session 4: "Low-Level Interfaces"

Leonid Ryzhyk, Ihor Kuz, and Gernot Heiser.
